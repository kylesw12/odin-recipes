Grilled Chicken Recipe

Description

This a more of a technique to make grilled chicken which tastes delicious, has a good source of protein, and is easy to make. It is a perfect recipe, for college students, like me!

Ingredients

  • However many chicken breast you plan to cook
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Any other seasonings you would like to add

Steps

  1. Wash chicken and place on a paper towl to dry
  2. Pat dry with more paper towl. The trick to delicious chicken breast is to cook it without moisture on the outside.
  3. Place chicken on a cutting board. If some pieces are too thick (over an inch thick) fillet them in half
  4. Heat your pan on the stove to medium to medium-low heat. My dorm stove isn't very hot so I put it on medium.
  5. Season chicken with all of your seasonings. First add salt, then the rest of your seasonings. Completely cover the chicken until it is a different color
  6. Before placing your chicken on the stove, spray your pan with vegetable oil. We do this now so the oil doesn't burn.
  7. Pat dry your chicken one last time since the salt will bring more moisture out of the chciken, and place your chicken on the pan.
  8. Try to leave the chicken alone until one side has a nice crust on it. Then flip one time. When they both have a crust, your chicken is ready!
