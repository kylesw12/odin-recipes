Grandma's Chicken Soup

Description

Are you feeling under the weather? This chicken soup is assured to help you feel better when Grandma is too busy. Even if aren't sick, freeze this recipe so when the time does come you'll have emergency soup when you need it!

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 lb) whole chicken
  • 1 lb of carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 lb of celery, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 bunch of fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of pepper
  • 10 cups of warm water as needed

Steps

  1. Clean chicken and place in large saucepan with carrots, celery, tomatoes, and onion
  2. Add dill sprigs and season with salt and pepper. Add water until chicken is submerged by 2 inches
  3. Cook over med-low head until chicken is cooked through ~3 hrs. Remove chicken to plate
  4. Remove meat from bones, cut into bite sized pieces and return to saucepan. Cook for ~30mins longer
  5. Remove dill and serve. Enjoy!
