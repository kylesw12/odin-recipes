Overnight Oats Recipe

Description

This is a delicious recipe for overnight oats which are easy to make the night before and a great source of early morning protein in the morning. With minimal calories, this meal is great for weight loss and building muscle. Additionally, this meal will leave you satisfied for hours, making it easier to resist snacking throughout the day!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cups of oats
  • 3/4 cups of greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1/2 scoop of protein powder (optional)
  • Drizzle of honey (optional)
  • Fresh fruit for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour 2/3 cups of oats into a cup or mason jar
  2. Scoop 3/4 cups of any greek yogurt, I recommend sugar free chobani, into cup
  3. Pour 1/2 a scoop of protein powder on top of the cup
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of water into jar
  5. Stir ingredients and keep in fridge for at least 4 hours
  6. When ready to eat, add toppings and enjoy!
